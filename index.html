Lionel Andres Messi

The legend, the myth.

Here's a Lionel Messi path career

  • 1987 - He was Born
  • 2000 - First trial with barcelona
  • 2003–2005 - Rise to the first team
  • 2005–2008 - Becoming a starting eleven player in barcelona
  • 2007–2008 - Copa America final and Olympic gold
  • 2009–2010 - First Ballon d'Or
  • 2010–2011 - Fifth La Liga title and third Champions League
  • 2012 - A record-breaking year
  • 2014–2015 - World Cup and Copa America finals
  • 2016–17 - Fourth Golden Boot
  • 2018–19 - Captaincy, 10th La Liga title, and a record sixth Golden Boot
  • 2019–20 - Record sixth Ballon d'Or
  • 2020 - Desire to leave Barcelona
  • 2020–21 - Final season at Barcelona
  • 2021–22 - Messi joined French club Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).
  • 2021–2022 - Copa America and World Cup triumphs
  • 2023–present - 100 international goals

“Every year I try to grow as a player and not get stuck in a rut. I try to improve my game in every way possible.”

– Lionel Messi
